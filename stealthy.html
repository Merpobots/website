The Original Vision

"When filming at concerts, ballets and other theater performances that allow recording, the screen is always so bright and probably annoying to other attendees. I want an app that allows rear camera recording while blacking out the screen almost entirely except for a small record button and a small low resolution view of the video. The buttons should be small and a deep red that is hard to see unless you are near the screen."

Project Vision Statement
The Problem

Traditional camera apps are disruptive in sensitive environments:

  • Bright screens distract performers and audience
  • Complex interfaces require attention
  • Shutter sounds break immersion
  • Large previews block the actual view

You want to capture memories without being "that person" with the bright phone.

Our Solution

Stealthy delivers true discretion:

  • Ultra-dim interface - Deep red theme with adjustable brightness
  • Minimal preview - Small, low-visibility video window
  • Complete silence - No shutter sounds or focus beeps
  • Gesture controls - Zoom and adjust without visible buttons
  • Professional quality - Full-resolution recording despite small preview
